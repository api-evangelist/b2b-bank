# B2B Bank (b2b-bank)

B2B Bank is a Schedule I Canadian bank, headquartered in Toronto and wholly owned by Laurentian Bank of Canada (Laurentian Bank Financial Group). It runs a business-to-business, intermediary-only model — manufacturing and distributing banking products through a network of roughly 27,000 financial professionals (advisors and their dealerships, deposit and mortgage brokers and their firms, mutual fund and insurance manufacturers, and CIRO members) rather than serving retail consumers directly.

## APIs

B2B Bank exposes **no public developer API**. There is no first-party developer portal — `developer.b2bbank.com` and `api.b2bbank.com` do not resolve (DNS failure), and the public website (`b2bbank.com`) surfaces only login-gated advisor and broker applications: Advisor Access, EASE (online loans), Group Access, Investor Access, and online banking. No downloadable OpenAPI/Swagger specification is published, so no specs were harvested.

## Open-Finance Posture

- **First-party developer portal:** None confirmed (developer/api subdomains do not resolve; 200-only responses come from gated advisor/broker portals, not developer APIs).
- **Consumer-Driven Banking (CDB):** Canada's federal Consumer-Driven Banking framework (Budget 2024 / Fall Economic Statement 2024, overseen by the FCAC) is legislated but not yet operational. B2B Bank publishes no stated CDB or FDX position.
- **Canadian rails:** No documented Interac data-sharing / verification API or Payments Canada Real-Time Rail (RTR) developer surface of its own is published.
- **Aggregator access:** No first-party aggregator (Flinks, Plaid, MX, Salt Edge) program is documented. As an intermediary bank, its digital surface is partner- and advisor-gated rather than developer-facing.

This is an identity-only (built-stub) record: an honest "no public API" result for an intermediary Schedule I bank.
